Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to own GTA 5 to play?

Yes, you need a legitimate copy of GTA 5 for PC to play on our FiveM server.

Is there an age requirement?

Players must be 18+ to join our server due to mature content and roleplay scenarios.

Do I need a microphone?

Yes, a working microphone is required for proper roleplay communication.

How do I apply for whitelisted jobs?

After joining, you can apply for whitelisted jobs through our Discord server.

Are there custom vehicles and items?

Yes, our server features many custom vehicles, weapons, and items not found in the base game.

How often are server updates?

We update the server weekly with new content and bug fixes.
