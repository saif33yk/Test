Server Rules

To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone, please follow these rules while playing on our server.

WELCOME TO GTA5RP

Our server is dedicated to providing the most immersive roleplay experience possible. The rules listed below are designed to ensure that all players can enjoy their time on the server.

Remember that the primary goal is to have fun while creating interesting stories and scenarios with other players. Respect the roleplay experience of others, and they will respect yours.

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on the severity and frequency of the violations. If you have any questions about the rules, please contact a staff member for clarification.

General Rules

Respect All Players

Treat all players with respect. Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.

No Cheating or Exploiting

Using cheats, hacks, or exploiting bugs is strictly prohibited and will result in an immediate ban.

Follow Staff Instructions

Staff members are here to help. Follow their instructions and treat them with respect.

No Metagaming

Using information your character wouldn't know in-game is prohibited (e.g., information from Discord or streams).

No Powergaming

Forcing scenarios on other players or performing unrealistic actions is not allowed.

Roleplay Rules

Stay In Character

Maintain your character's persona at all times while in-game. Use appropriate channels for OOC (Out of Character) communication.

Value Your Life

Act as if your character values their life. Don't make unrealistic decisions in dangerous situations.

No Random Death Match (RDM)

Killing players without valid roleplay reason is prohibited.

No Vehicle Death Match (VDM)

Using vehicles as weapons without roleplay context is not allowed.

Realistic Roleplay

Keep your actions realistic. If you wouldn't do it in real life, your character probably shouldn't either.

Criminal Activity Rules

Robbery Cooldowns

After participating in a robbery, you must wait 1 hour before participating in another.

No Cop Baiting

Intentionally provoking police officers without a roleplay reason is prohibited.

Hostage Rules

You may not take more than 5 hostages at once. Hostage situations should not exceed 30 minutes.

Gang Territory

Respect established gang territories. Conflicts over territory must have proper roleplay context.

Illegal Business

All illegal businesses must maintain secrecy and proper roleplay. No advertising illegal services in public.

Rule Enforcement

Our staff team enforces these rules to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all players. Consequences for breaking rules may include:

  • Verbal warnings
  • Temporary mute or jail
  • Temporary ban (1-30 days)
  • Permanent ban for severe or repeated violations

If you believe a rule has been enforced unfairly, you may appeal through our Discord server.
