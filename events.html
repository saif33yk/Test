Event Rules & Guidelines

Registration

  • Register for events through our Discord or in-game.
  • Some events have limited slots and require early registration.
  • Registration closes 1 hour before the event starts.
  • No-shows may be penalized for future event registrations.

Participation

  • Be online at least 15 minutes before the event starts.
  • Follow staff instructions during the event.
  • Maintain roleplay standards during events.
  • Cheating or exploiting will result in disqualification and possible ban.

Prizes

  • Prizes are awarded at the end of each event.
  • Some prizes may be delivered within 24 hours after the event.
  • Prize distribution is final and determined by event staff.
  • Unclaimed prizes expire after 7 days.

Streaming

  • Streaming events is allowed and encouraged.
  • Use the hashtag #GTA5RP when streaming our events.
  • Some events may have special streaming rules.
  • Featured streamers may receive additional perks.
